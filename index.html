About and Tech Stack

Specialized in shaping lightweight models, including Microsoft Phi-4 variants, into hyper-efficient tools for developer workflows, local inference, and practical on-device automation.

KodeCharya focuses on small-model optimization with a strong bias toward real-world utility. The work centers on instruction tuning, compact reasoning behavior, and deployment paths that stay fast on consumer hardware. The result is a toolkit of models that can support coding, debugging, and software design without depending on remote APIs.
